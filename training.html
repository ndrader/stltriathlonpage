Workout Guide

How to Use the Workout Guide:

There will be 4 Phases of training leading up to the St. Louis Triathlon, lasting 4 month or 16 weeks. The phases include: Base phase (6 weeks), build phase (5 weeks), specialty phase (4 weeks), and taper (1 week). For each phase, there will be a weekly template which will show you the general weekly structure; there will also be weekly updates which is to be used with the general template in order to progress your fitness. Follow your current training phaseâ€™ template for every week of that training phase, then read the weekly updates for changes to your training from week to week.

How to Adjust for Missed Workouts:

  • If you missed 1-4 days, skip the days that you missed, and continue with the plan.
  • If you missed 5-7 days, disregard the main set for the workout and swim or bike or run easy for 2 days, then continue with plan as instructed.
  • If you miss 1 week or more, repeat the previous week of training, and skip the last week of the training phase that you are on.

Base Training Phase (6 weeks) Overview and Goals

  • Improve cardiovascular fitness
  • Increase bone density and joint durability to handle the build phase training
  • Training intensity for most workouts will be moderate
  • Improve body composition (If needed)
  • Develop the habit of consistency
